About Me

"Go forth to serve."

I grew up in Gilbert, Arizona, where I was encouraged to explore any and all of my interests. I was involved in theater, choir, art, politics, religion, and more. Being involved in the community and with various artistic pursuits helped open my eyes to the world.

After a year in college, I took time off to serve on an eighteen-month volunteer mission in Kentucky. While there, I served the people of that great state and learned more about the effects of poverty, addiction, and hardship.

Here is a short video of the recent Kentucky tornadoes, just to show the kind of storms they have been facing..

And here is a link to more information about Kentucky relief efforts.

All of this has left me with the desire to serve and lift up the communities I live in. There are opportunities to serve and love everywhere I look, and I intend to take every single one of them.
